What Is SolSuites?

SolSuites is a metaverse project built on the Solana blockchain. Our mission is to create a luxurious apartment experience in the metaverse, which comes equipped with a rooftop patio, lounge, NFT gallery, entertainment centre, NFT clothing/furniture store, and more.

Use your 3D NFT's as avatars or create your own with our built in avatar creator. Chat and make friends, customize your suite, host community events, and just have a great time overall.

When mint goes live, your NFT will be your proof of ownership and ticket to your space in the building. Your ticket will then be swapped to a keycard that will allow you to access your suite.

What do I get?

During the mint stage (0.8 Sol) you will get an NFT ticket. This ticket will secure your suite for when we start developing SolSuites. Once the project begins development, we will then start preparing for a swap where you will be assigned your suite. We want the community involved as much as possible when it comes to the design of the building and layout, so be sure to join our discord to take part in it.

What’s an NFT?

NFT stands for "Non-fungible token," which means that it's a unique, digital item with blockchain-managed ownership that users can buy, own, and trade. Some NFT's fundamental function is to be digital art. But they can also offer additional benefits like exclusive access to websites, event tickets, game items, and ownership records for physical objects. Think of it as a unique piece of art that can also work as a "members-only" card. SolSuite works like this.

How do I buy?

SolSuites is on the Solana blockchain, so in order to mint or buy you will need Solana and a compatible wallet.

  1. Download the Phantom wallet extension for the Chrome/Brave browser or app on mobile.
  2. You can purchase Solana through the Phantom Wallet or Send Solana from an exchange like Coinbase.
  3. Click on Connect at the top of the mint page and connect your Phantom Wallet. Once joined, you will be able to purchase the NFTs in the mint section. You will be prompted to sign your transaction. FYI, there will be a fee associated with every transaction related to gas prices but the beauty of Solana is that the fee is very minimal.
  4. Once you have made your purchase, your SolSuite NFT will be viewable in your wallet.
